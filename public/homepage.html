Toycathon 2021

Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences has been selected as Nodal Centre, Mumbai, to host the Grand Finale of Toycathon 2021. Toycathon 2021 is an inter-ministerial initiative organized by the Ministry of Education's Innovation Cell with support from multiple ministries.

Top 2% Scientists (Stanford University)

Four faculty members of the Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences (KITS), Coimbatore, have been featured in the top 2% of scientists listed by Stanford University, USA.

Selection Trials for Sports Quota 2024-25

Sports quota selection trials for academic year 2024–2025 for various games, including athletics, are scheduled from 15th to 21st May 2024 in the institution sports complex.

Innovations in Online Teaching, Learning and Evaluation (IOTLE 2021)

KITS is organizing a 3-day International Webinar titled "Innovations in Online Teaching, Learning and Evaluation" (IOTLE 2021) starting from 05 August 2021 for continuous improvement.
